## Screenshot Feedback

### Home Desktop
Status: `Not yet 100%`

- The hero is much stronger, but the right image stage still feels a little too isolated from the copy. It is premium, not unforgettable.
- The `Signature Services` slab is finally cohesive, but the lower gold card still feels like a nice insert instead of a decisive focal accent.
- The audience section is structurally clear, but the first pale panel is still slightly too polite compared to the dark operator block.
- The final CTA band is better, but the right side still looks under-used.

### Services Desktop
Status: `Close, but not 100%`

- The hero composition is now strong and the side rail finally looks integrated.
- The service deck reads far better than the earlier big-dark-box version, but the pale left intro is still a little too passive.
- The `Premium service starts with discipline` strip is clearer, but still reads more like a clean layout row than a luxurious proof moment.
- The bottom split is good, but the page still peaks in the top half.

### Capabilities Desktop
Status: `Not yet 100%`

- The hero has improved, but the right systems block still feels more like notes than a formidable proof device.
- The `Capability is as much about process quality as technical depth` strip is too thin and too gentle for the page’s mission.
- The operations section is better, but the pale left text block still feels slightly underpowered next to the photo.
- The page overall is competent, but still not severe enough.

### Ownership Desktop
Status: `Not yet 100%`

- The hero reads clearly, but the page still drops into brochure mode after the first dark split.
- The `Exceptional aircraft deserve care...` section is too safe for how emotionally important it is.
- The expectation map is useful, but the composition still feels like a refined content block instead of a real signature moment.
- The close section contains visibly weak blank support boxes and fails outright in this screenshot.

### About Desktop
Status: `Close, but not 100%`

- The hero and manifesto area are much stronger and finally feel like one authored system.
- The values ledger is more legible now, but still slightly too quiet relative to the hero.
- The `Behavior is the real brand language` section is finally visible, but it remains a touch too rectangular and system-like.
- The page is good, but still lacks one almost shocking, brand-defining composition.

### Contact Desktop
Status: `Pass with minor reservations`

- The intake console is coherent and premium now.
- The right support rail feels integrated instead of tacked on.
- The process section is much stronger, though it still wants a little more tension.
- This is the closest page to complete.

### Home Mobile
Status: `Not yet 100%`

- Mobile rhythm is better, but still too dependent on dark block followed by dark block.
- The opening stack is cleaner, but still slightly too uniform.
- The lower sections are readable, though the page still feels like a compressed desktop composition rather than a truly authored mobile sequence.

### Services Mobile
Status: `Close, but not 100%`

- The page is materially stronger than earlier versions.
- The hero and featured service sequence hold up well.
- Mobile still stacks multiple dark sections too consecutively.
- The page needs one stronger visual pause or asymmetry moment in the lower half.

### Capabilities Mobile
Status: `Fail`

- The page contains a broken-feeling empty pale panel in the operations section.
- That empty block destroys trust because it reads like missing content rather than intended space.
- The page also stacks too many pale technical strips in similar widths afterward.
- This screenshot does not meet the standard.

### Ownership Mobile
Status: `Not yet 100%`

- The page is readable, but the middle becomes too list-like and brochure-like.
- The expectation section is useful, but still feels stacked rather than composed.
- The close section still shows weak blank support boxes and fails visually.

### About Mobile
Status: `Close, but not 100%`

- The mobile manifesto section is much better.
- The page carries authority, but the values stretch is still somewhat repetitive.
- It needs one more cadence change to feel truly luxurious instead of just disciplined.

### Contact Mobile
Status: `Pass with minor reservations`

- The form hierarchy is much better and no longer looks generic.
- The pacing is improved and the intake process reads clearly.
- It still could use a little more emotional release after the form, but it is credible now.

## Immediate Fix List From Round 1

1. Rebuild the broken `Capabilities` mobile operations section so there is no empty panel and the composition survives the stack.
2. Fix the blank support boxes in the `Ownership` close section on desktop and mobile.
3. Make `Ownership` less brochure-like in the middle of the page.
4. Tighten `Home` mobile rhythm so it stops repeating dark-block cadence too predictably.
5. Give `Capabilities` a stronger proof strip so the page feels technically formidable rather than simply clean.
